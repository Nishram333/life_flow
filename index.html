About Blood Donation

Why Give Blood?

Blood donation is a vital lifeline for many people needing long-term treatments, emergency care, and complex surgeries. Your donation can help save lives and improve the health of countless patients.

Who Can Donate?

Generally, anyone aged 17-65, weighing over 50kg, and in good health can donate blood. Donors should have normal blood pressure, hemoglobin levels, and be free from infections.

Donation Process

The entire process takes about 30-45 minutes, with the actual blood donation only taking 8-10 minutes. All equipment used is sterile and disposed of after single use.

How Often?

You can donate whole blood every 12 weeks. This interval allows your body to replenish iron stores and maintain good health while helping others regularly.

Safety First

All donations undergo rigorous testing for blood-borne diseases. Our facilities maintain the highest standards of safety and hygiene in collection and storage.
